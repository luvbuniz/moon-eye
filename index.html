🌙 Moon Eye
A sparkly building world for dreamers

Build pink houses in the sky, plant cherry blossoms & bamboo, pet foxes and pandas, eat yummy food, and fill your world with love. Stack blocks up to the clouds — then put your house on top!

How to play
• W A S D move · Space jump · mouse look
• Left click place block · Right click break
• 1–9 hotbar · E eat selected food
• Search button shows everything in the game
• Near water? Place more water & grass to grow your world!
